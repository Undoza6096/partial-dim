NG+3 Respecced

Created by Aarex Tiaokhiao

NG+3 Respecced is a modification of Antimatter Dimensions which adds a lot of end-game content.
To play, click "Load" in the options tab, click "Mods" on the load menu, click "NG++" button until you see "NG+++", and then click "New save!"
(recommended for people who experienced my mod site before)

You can visit this wiki about this mod at http://ng-plus-plus-plus.fandom.com.
